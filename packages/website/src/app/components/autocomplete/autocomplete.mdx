# Autocomplete

Autocomplete component enhances user interaction by facilitating the swift selection of options from extensive lists.
Suitable for a wide range of applications where a selection is required, such as choosing a country, organisation or product.
Advised to be used in place of dropdowns of 10 items or more.

[View this component on GitHub](https://github.com/UKHO/admiralty-design-system/tree/main/packages/core/src/components/autocomplete)
